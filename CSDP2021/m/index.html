CSDP 2021

3rd International Workshop on Cyber Security and Data Privacy
Aug. 19 - Aug. 20 2021
Virtual, China

As the information technologies are adopted in our daily life, security and privacy become more and more important. The boundaries of cyber world and physical world are being changed, as well as the research topics of security and privacy. In order to solve the emerging problems of security and privacy, we need to bring intelligent and professional people together, which are with different backgrounds and from different organizations.

The 3rd International Workshop on Cybersecurity and Data Privacy (CSDP) aims to provide a forum for people to discuss the problems, the challenges, the solutions and the envisions of security and privacy in the cyberspace.
